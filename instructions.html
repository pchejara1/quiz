Instructions

Welcome to the Sports Quiz! Test your knowledge of the sporting world and challenge yourself with a series of exciting questions.

  1. Categories: The quiz is divided into different categories, each focusing on a specific sport or aspect of sports. Choose your favorite category or try them all!
  2. Multiple-Choice Questions: Each category contains multiple-choice questions. Read the question carefully and select the correct answer from the options provided.
  3. Time Limit: You'll have a specified time limit to answer each question. Stay focused and think quickly to beat the clock!
  4. Scoring: Points are awarded for each correct answer. The faster you answer, the more points you'll earn. Aim for a high score and challenge yourself to reach the top of the leaderboard.
  5. Hint System: If you're stuck on a question, don't worry! You'll have access to a hint system that can provide helpful clues to guide you towards the right answer.
  6. Final Score: At the end of the quiz, you'll receive your final score, along with a breakdown of how you performed in each category. Share your score with friends and see who knows sports best!
  7. Play Again: Loved the quiz? Play again to improve your score and discover new sports trivia.
